Who We Are

Emmanuel Ministers is a vibrant, Christ-centered youth ministry based at Kamnara SDA Church. We believe in serving God through music, prayer, outreach, and fellowship. Our members are passionate about spreading the gospel and supporting each other in faith.

Our Mission

To empower the youth to discover their purpose in Christ and to lead a life of integrity, service, and worship.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of Christ-like leaders who influence the world through love, truth, and spiritual boldness.
